Utilities
=========

The following Utilities are used for technical purposes and are not required for the basic usage of Promptzl.
However, to understand the most important utilities of this package, the most essential utilities are listed below.
The most important function is the :meth:`~promptzl.utils.calibrate` function, which is used to calibrate the
probabilities, often leading to better performance. More details about calibration are described in :ref:`calibration`.
